As of API 22, the `AlarmManager` will override near-future and high-frequency alarm requests, delaying the alarm at least 5 seconds into the future and ensuring that the repeat interval is at least 60 seconds. If you really need to do work sooner than 5 seconds, post a delayed message or runnable to a Handler.
